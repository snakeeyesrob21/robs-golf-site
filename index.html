Meet Your Coach

Rob L Rossi

Rob L Rossi

Head Golf Instructor

With 35 years immersed in the game of golf and over 15 years of dedicated instruction experience, Rob Rossi has established himself as Northern Nevada's premier golf coach. His innovative teaching methodology combines classical fundamentals with cutting-edge swing analysis technology to deliver measurable results for golfers at every level.

Rob's passion for the game extends beyond technique—he believes in building complete golfers who understand course management, mental strategy, and the discipline required for continuous improvement. His students have gone on to win club championships, dramatically lower their handicaps, and simply discover a deeper love for the game.

Based in Reno and serving the greater Northern Nevada region, Rob brings a personalized approach to every lesson, ensuring that each student receives instruction tailored to their unique swing characteristics and golfing goals.
